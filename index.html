Why only £99 upfront?
Because you should see what you’re getting before you pay the bulk of it. The £99 covers the brief, design and build. Once you’re happy, you pay the balance — only then does it go live. No risk, no nasty surprises.
How does the final payment work?
When your site is ready and approved, I send one secure payment link. A single click covers the £400 balance and sets up your £19/month care plan at the same time — card saved once, monthly runs automatically. Nothing complicated.
What does the £19/month cover?
Everything to keep your site working and winning: hosting, domain, security, small content updates — plus I monitor your uptime and test your enquiry form, so every lead reaches you. And I keep you visible on Google and AI as things change. No contract — cancel any time, and your site stays live for the rest of the paid month.
How long does a build take?
Fast — typically within 24 hours of receiving your brief and photos. I’ll confirm the timeline when we speak.
Do I own the website?
Yes. The content and design are yours. I manage the hosting and domain as part of the £19/month — if you ever cancel, I’ll hand over all the files and transfer the domain within 14 days. No lock-in.
Can you handle bookings, shops or menus?
For most local businesses the standard £499 build covers everything. If you need online bookings, a shop or a restaurant menu system, mention it when you enquire — I’ll quote separately, but it’s very doable.
